Our Story

Founded in the late 1970s as Allstate Permit Services, we've spent over four decades helping bring responsible energy development to American land. Rebranded as APS Land to better reflect the full scope of what we do best — from deep title work to fair negotiations and careful project support.

We're Western landowners ourselves. We understand property rights, the importance of good stewardship, and what it means to keep your word on every deal.
